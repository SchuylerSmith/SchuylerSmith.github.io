I am currently a Ph.D. student studying Bioinformatics and Computation Biology in the Department of Agriculture and Biosystems Engineering at Iowa State University

I am a member of the GERMS lab, PI-ed by Dr. Adina Howe. Our lab focuses on research with environmental microbiomes; predominantly soil, but also water systems, manure, and even flower nectar.

In my free time I enjoy reading fantasy novels, playing games, going to the gym, and learning how to build unnecessarily fancy websites about myself.

I have also been fortunate to travel to various parts of the world in recent years. The more exotic places were on birding trips with my father, like the background picture I am using that I took at the Canopy Tower in Panama. I hope to document parts of these trips in my Photos tab, meanwhile, some pictures are featured on my Instagram.
